WEDGE_EXACTNESS
Precision Test for Wedge Quadrature Rules

WEDGE_EXACTNESS is a FORTRAN90 program which measures the precision of a quadrature rule over the interior of the unit wedge in 3D.

The interior of the unit wedge in 3D is defined by the constraints: 

        0 <= X
        0 <= Y
             X + Y <= 1
       -1 <= Z <= +1

Usage:

wedge_exactness filename degree_max
where
  • filename is the common prefix of the filenames containing the abscissas and the weights of the quadrature rule.
  • degree_max is the maximum degree of the monomials to be checked.

Related Data and Programs:

CUBE_EXACTNESS, a MATLAB library which investigates the polynomial exactness of quadrature rules over the interior of a cube in 3D.

HYPERCUBE_EXACTNESS, a MATLAB program which measures the monomial exactness of an M-dimensional quadrature rule over the interior of the unit hypercube in M dimensions.

PYRAMID_EXACTNESS, a MATLAB program which investigates the polynomial exactness of a quadrature rule over the interior of the unit pyramid in 3D.

SPHERE_EXACTNESS, a MATLAB program which tests the polynomial exactness of a quadrature rule over the surface of the unit sphere in 3D.

SQUARE_EXACTNESS, a MATLAB library which investigates the polynomial exactness of quadrature rules for f(x,y) over the interior of a rectangle in 2D.

TETRAHEDRON_EXACTNESS, a MATLAB program which investigates the polynomial exactness of a quadrature rule over the interior of a tetrahedron in 3D.

TRIANGLE_EXACTNESS, a MATLAB program which investigates the polynomial exactness of a quadrature rule over the interior of a triangle in 2D.

WEDGE_FELIPPA_RULE, a MATLAB library which returns quadratures rules for approximating integrals over the interior of the unit wedge in 3D.

WEDGE_GRID, a MATLAB library which computes a grid of points over the interior of the unit wedge in 3D.

WEDGE_INTEGRALS, a MATLAB library which returns the exact value of the integral of any monomial over the interior of the unit wedge in 3D.

WEDGE_MONTE_CARLO, a MATLAB library which uses the Monte Carlo method to estimate integrals over the interior of the unit wedge in 3D.
